\section{Profile}

Bioinformatician specialising in reproducible, scalable pipelines for
evolutionary and developmental genomics. I build Nextflow/nf-core
workflows for high-throughput sequencing data --- RNA-Seq, MS-proteomics
and Hi-C --- and currently develop open-source pipelines for evolution
and ecology at UCL, where I built \textbf{Eco-Flow}, a community working
to close ``the omics gap in ecology''. Its growing collection spans
comparative and ecological genomics: genome quality assessment
(\texttt{genomeqc}), synteny analysis (\texttt{synteny}),
transposable-element and gene-family evolution (\texttt{comparete},
\texttt{excon}), BUSCO-based phylogenomics (\texttt{buscophylo}) and DNA
metabarcoding. A fluent programmer (Unix, R, Python, Perl) and confident
science communicator, with high-impact publications stemming from a PhD
on alternative splicing at the Centre for Genomic Regulation (CRG,
Barcelona).
