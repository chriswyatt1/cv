% ---- Profile --------------------------------------------------------
\section{Profile}

Bioinformatician specialising in reproducible, scalable pipelines for
evolutionary and developmental genomics. I build Nextflow/nf-core
workflows for high-throughput sequencing data --- RNA-Seq, genomics and proteomics
--- and currently develop open-source pipelines for evolution
and ecology at UCL, where I built \textbf{Eco-Flow}, a community working
to close ``the omics gap in ecology''. Its growing collection spans
comparative and ecological genomics, from genome quality assessment and
synteny analysis to gene-family evolution and DNA metabarcoding. A fluent
programmer (Unix, R, Python, Perl) and an active science communicator and
trainer, with high-impact publications stemming from a PhD
on alternative splicing at the Centre for Genomic Regulation (CRG,
Barcelona).
